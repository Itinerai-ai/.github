# Itinerai

**Travel, reimagined by AI.**

[Itinerai](https://itinerai.ai) is an AI-powered travel platform that connects travelers directly with hotels, flights and transfer services — with no OTA commissions.

## What is Itinerai?

Itinerai is a next-generation travel platform built on conversational AI. Instead of browsing endless listings, you simply tell Itinerai what you're looking for — and our AI concierge finds the best options for you.

Whether you're searching for a boutique hotel in Rome, a flight to Barcelona, or a private transfer from the airport, Itinerai handles it all in one place.

## Why Itinerai?

- **No commissions.** Itinerai connects you directly with hotels and service providers, cutting out OTA middlemen.
- **AI-powered search.** Our conversational AI understands what you actually want — not just keywords.
- **Hotels, flights & transfers.** Everything you need to plan a trip, in one platform.
- **Personalized recommendations.** Itinerai learns your preferences and tailors results to how you travel.
- **Coverage across Europe.** From Italy to Spain, France to Greece — Itinerai covers the destinations you love.

## How it works

1. **Chat with Itinerai** — Tell our AI concierge where you want to go and what you need.
2. **Get personalized results** — Itinerai searches thousands of options and recommends the best matches.
3. **Book directly** — No intermediaries, no hidden fees. You deal directly with the property or service provider.

## Built with

Itinerai is built using modern technologies including Next.js, Python, and advanced RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) systems for intelligent travel recommendations.
